# Literature Map

## Dwarf-galaxy halo diversity

- Core-cusp and diversity problem papers.
- Rotation-curve diagnostics, especially dwarf and low-surface-brightness
  systems.
- SPARC-like data comparisons for later phases.

## Baryonic feedback and halo response

- Gas outflows, bursty feedback, star-formation thresholds.
- Black-hole and cosmic-ray contributions in dwarf simulations.
- Dark matter profile changes from baryon-driven potential fluctuations.

## Subgrid and closure modeling

- Effective theories in IllustrisTNG, EAGLE, FIRE, and related simulations.
- Turbulence closure and cosmic-ray diffusion subgrid models.
- Parameter discipline: calibration, transferability, and failure criteria.

## ASF-specific gaps

- Derive or justify `S_A` from coarse-grained dynamics.
- Show ASF is not just arbitrary curve fitting.
- Identify observables that distinguish ASF from ordinary feedback tuning.
